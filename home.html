Ariel Jonathan Roffé

Hi! I'm Ariel Roffé. I'm a philosophy reasearcher from Argentina. Blablabla.

My research areas are the logic of science and the philosophy of biology (especially of systematics). I also develop computational applications for philosophers. I received my PhD from the University of Buenos Aires in 2020, I have a research scholarship from CONICET, and a teaching position at the Department of Philosophy of the University of Buenos Aires.

I am a member of the Grupo ANFIBIO, the Centro de Filosofía e Historia de la Ciencia and the BA-Logic Group, as well as other research groups in the philosophy of science and in mathematical logic.
